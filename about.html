Who are we

Meta Chemicals & Machinery (MCM) delivers industrial-grade solutions in automation, security, safety, and operational monitoring for sectors such as oil & gas, heavy industries, infrastructure, and public institutions. We integrate smart systems, AI, IoT, and engineering technologies to improve safety, efficiency, and regulatory compliance.

Our Mission & Core Values

Our Mission

To accelerate the digital transformation of industrial and enterprise operations in Azerbaijan and the wider region — delivering reliable, cutting-edge technology solutions that create measurable value for our clients.

Our Vision

To become the leading technology integration partner for critical infrastructure and industrial sectors across the South Caucasus — recognized for engineering excellence, innovation, and long-term client partnerships.

Our Values

Engineering integrity, client-first accountability, continuous innovation, and a commitment to safety and regulatory compliance in every project we deliver — regardless of scale or complexity.
